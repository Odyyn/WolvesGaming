Counter-Strike: Global Offensive 2

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a renowned multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Valve Corporation. Players compete as terrorists or counter-terrorists in objective-based modes on various maps, purchasing weapons and equipment each round. With a vibrant esports scene, an in-game economy for trading weapon skins, and regular updates from Valve, CS:GO maintains its popularity among gamers worldwide.
