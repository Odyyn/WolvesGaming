League Of Legends

It's a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game developed and published by Riot Games. In League of Legends, players assume the role of a "champion" with unique abilities and engage in team-based matches against other players or computer-controlled champions. The objective is typically to destroy the opposing team's Nexus, a structure located within their base, while defending their own. The game has gained a massive player base and is widely regarded as one of the most influential and successful esports titles.
