DOTA 2

Dota 2 is a popular multiplayer online battle arena game developed by Valve Corporation, featuring teams of heroes competing to destroy each other's Ancient. With a vast roster of unique heroes, strategic gameplay on a symmetrical map, and a thriving esports scene highlighted by The International tournament, Dota 2 maintains a dedicated community and receives regular updates from Valve to keep the game engaging and competitive.
